3. Peach Eyes

By Wave to Earth


Album:0.1 Flaws and All


Year Released: 2023


The song emphasizes the singer's emotional refuge in their partner, with lines expressing a desire to “hide inside your eyes” to escape life’s uncertainties. The repeated phrase “Would you don’t mind?” reflects a longing for reciprocated affection and a fear of rejection, underscoring the vulnerability and commitment in the relationship.


Overall, “Peach Eyes” is a tender ode to love, illustrating how the partner’s gaze provides a sense of stability and solace, making them a central source of support and inspiration for the singer​

10. Off to the Races

Artist: Lana Del Rey


Album: Born To Die


Year Released: 2011


This song has Lana professing her love for her rich, gangster boyfriend. However, their Bonnie & Clyde-like relationship is doomed.


Lana portrays a character (which is a hybrid of her own image and Lolita) that has a criminal past of alcohol, stripping and overspending. She falls in love with a wealthy gangster who has a profound love for women, cocaine, and money.
